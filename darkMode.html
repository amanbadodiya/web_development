About

Sant Singaji Educational Society (SSES) was established in 2010 to bring social and economic transformation in rural areas of Madhya Pradesh. It runs Sant Singaji Institute of Science and Management in Sandalpur, Dewas district and educates around 1000 students every year.

Services

  1. Education designed for Rural Youth
  2. Practical Industry Exposure
  3. Special Classes for Students
  4. English and IT Skills
  5. Career Counselling
  6. Social Awareness Campaigns
