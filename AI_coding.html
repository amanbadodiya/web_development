About Us

Sant Singaji Educational Society (SSES) was established in 2010 to bring social and economic transformation in rural areas of Madhya Pradesh. It runs Sant Singaji Institute of Science and Management in Sandalpur, Dewas district — educating around 1,000 students every year with a focus on practical skills and real-world readiness.
